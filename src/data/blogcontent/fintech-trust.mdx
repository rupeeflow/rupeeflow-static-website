## Trust is the Foundation of Fintech

User confidence is essential for the growth of financial technology.

### How Trust is Built

- transparent pricing  
- regulatory compliance  
- secure data handling  

### Role of Regulations

Governments enforce compliance to ensure user safety and financial stability.

### The Future

Fintech companies that prioritize transparency will gain long-term customer loyalty.
