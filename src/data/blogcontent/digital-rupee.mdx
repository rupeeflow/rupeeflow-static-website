## India’s Digital Rupee Initiative

The Reserve Bank of India is developing a Central Bank Digital Currency (CBDC).

### Key Benefits

- reduced cash dependency  
- faster settlements  
- enhanced transparency  

### Use Cases

Digital currency can streamline government payments, retail transactions, and cross-border settlements.

### What to Expect

CBDCs may redefine how money is stored, transferred, and regulated.
