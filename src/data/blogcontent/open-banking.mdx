## What is Open Banking?

Open banking allows financial institutions to share data securely via APIs.

### Benefits

- improved financial transparency  
- better financial products  
- enhanced customer control  

### Developer Innovation

APIs enable fintech developers to build smarter financial applications.

### Future Impact

Open banking will foster collaboration and innovation across the financial ecosystem.
