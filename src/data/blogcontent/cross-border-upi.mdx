## Cross-Border UPI is Changing Global Payments

India’s Unified Payments Interface (UPI) is expanding globally, enabling NRIs and international users to send and receive money instantly.

### Why Cross-Border UPI Matters

- eliminates high remittance fees  
- enables real-time transfers  
- simplifies payments for NRIs  

### Benefits for Users

UPI allows families, students, and professionals abroad to send funds home instantly without relying on traditional banking delays.

### Future Outlook

As adoption grows, cross-border UPI could redefine global remittances and financial accessibility.
