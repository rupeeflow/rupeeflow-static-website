## UPI’s Global Expansion

India’s UPI infrastructure is influencing payment ecosystems worldwide.

### Global Impact

- faster international payments  
- lower transaction costs  
- financial inclusion for emerging markets  

### Countries Exploring UPI Integration

Several countries are collaborating with India to enable UPI-based payment acceptance.

### What’s Next?

UPI has the potential to become a universal digital payment standard.
