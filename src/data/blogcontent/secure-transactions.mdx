## Understanding Digital Payment Security

With digital payments growing rapidly, security remains a top priority.

### Technologies Protecting Transactions

- end-to-end encryption  
- tokenization  
- multi-factor authentication  

### Regulatory Protection

Financial regulators enforce strict compliance measures to protect users.

### Staying Safe

Users should adopt secure practices such as strong passwords and device protection.
